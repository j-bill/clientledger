## üí° Key Insights from Codebase Analysis

### What Makes Freelance Helper Unique
1. **All-in-One Solution:** Time tracking + Projects + Invoicing + Analytics
2. **Security First:** Mandatory 2FA with QR codes, recovery codes, and trusted devices
3. **Beautiful Analytics:** Google Charts integration with real-time insights
4. **Team Ready:** Scales from solo freelancer to small agency
5. **Modern Tech:** Vue 3 SPA with glassmorphism design
6. **Flexible Billing:** Multiple hourly rates per project/user

### Target Market Validation
- Solo freelancers (primary)
- Small agencies (2-10 people)
- Consultants and contractors
- Anyone billing by the hour

### Competitive Positioning
- More comprehensive than simple time trackers
- More affordable than enterprise solutions
- Better security than spreadsheets
- Prettier than traditional invoicing software
